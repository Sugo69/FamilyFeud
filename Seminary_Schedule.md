# Seminary New Testament Schedule (2027)

## CFM vs Seminary — Key Distinctions

### Come Follow Me (tracked in `src/lib/cfm-schedule.js`)
- Calendar year — Jan 1 to Dec 31
- Weekly — 52/53 lessons per year
- Same content globally at the same time
- 2027 = New Testament for everyone, worldwide, Jan–Dec

### Seminary (separate system)
- 160 daily lessons across 36 weeks
- **No fixed calendar dates** — weeks 1–36 are placed by each teacher/program on their school year
- Curriculum year is still Jan–Dec (aligned to CFM since 2020 reform)

---

## Hemisphere Scheduling

| | North Hemisphere | South Hemisphere |
|---|---|---|
| School year | Aug/Sep – May/Jun | Jan/Feb – Oct/Nov |
| New curriculum starts | January 1 (mid-school-year) | January/February (start of school year) |
| 2027 NT coverage | Weeks 1–~18 (Jan–May 2027) | Weeks 1–36 (Jan–Oct 2027) |

**Note:** A North hemisphere student in school year 2026–2027 studies OT from Aug–Dec 2026, then switches to NT on Jan 1, 2027 and finishes ~May. They cover roughly half the 36-week NT curriculum in their school year.

## Implication for Kindred

Seminary and CFM are **two separate schedule shapes**:

- **CFM**: week → specific Sunday date (handled by `cfm-schedule.js`)
- **Seminary**: week-number 1–36 → scripture range only, no fixed dates

To support Seminary teachers, the best UI approach is either:
- A **"Seminary week" input** (1–36) that teachers enter manually
- **Auto-detection** based on hemisphere + current date (complex; different for North vs South)

The existing `cfm-schedule.js` "Fill This Week" button pattern does not translate directly to Seminary — there is no single global "current Seminary week."
